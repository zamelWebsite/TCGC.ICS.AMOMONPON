The Tangub City Global College Lux Mundi Cup 2024 was an experience I'll never forget. It wasn't just about the competition it was about learning, growing, and working together. I loved meeting new people, tackling challenges head-on, and celebrating every victory, big or small. The event taught me the value of resilience, preparation, and pushing myself beyond what I thought were my limits. It reminded me that success feels so much sweeter when it's shared and that real growth happens when you step out of your comfort zone.

Being part of the Cup showed me how much we can achieve when we support and believe in one another. Each challenge felt less daunting with a great team by my side, and every win brought us closer together. More than anything, it reinforced for me that hard work and perseverance really do pay off. The friendships and memories I made during this journey are something I'll carry with me forever.
